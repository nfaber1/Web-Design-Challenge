Latitude

Visual 1

Visual 1 shows that a city's latitude can be a factor in its max temperature. Latitude is one part in measuring specific geographic location going North to South.

Depending on how far north or south a city is positioned can generally determine how hot or cold it may be. A city located further north or south may typically be colder whereas a city situated near the equator will be hotter.
